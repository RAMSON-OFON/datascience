Effects of Loan features on ProsperScore¶

Investigation Overview¶

ProsperScore affects interest rate, loan amount and many other variables in our data set.

Objctives : In this investigation,I am interested in predicting features that determine prosper Score?.

Dataset Overview¶

The Prosper loan dataset comprises of 113937 loan entries with 81 attributes on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others, from the year 2009-2014. There are two main categories in this dataset:

Majority of the borrowers are employed, seconded by part time workers while some full time wokers. A few of them were self employed while very few were not employed.

Distribution of IncomeGroup, Employment status, Term over prosper score¶

As we can see bellow, High and middle income earners have a higher prosper score than lower class income earners. Full time workers, people who are employed, part time workers do have a higher score comapare to sel-employed and unemployed works. As expected, if your employed, you have a higher chance of paying your loan hence higher score. supriseingly, the prosper scores for short term, middle and long term loans are thesame. hence term of loan is not a determiant of the prosper score.

Prosperscore vs Empoyment status and incomeverifiable¶

There is a strong variation of prosperscore for prople who are Employed and not employed with veriable source of income ahaist those who are employed and not employed without a veriable shource of income.

Interesting to see that Proseperscore is thesame for full time and part time workers weather they have a verifiable source of income or not.

Hence, Employment status with verifiable income are huge determinants of prosperscore

Prosperscore, Loan category and Term¶

Household Expenses, Mdedical/Dental, Auto, Home improvement, large purchase with short term loans have a higher score comapred to those with middle and long term loans

Baby & Adoption, Engineering Ring, Motorcycle with Long term loans have a higher prosperscore comapred to middle and short term loans.

Green Loans where availbe only for middle and long term loans

Students have only Middle loans of 36 months available for their disposal

Hence, loan category and the Term of loan have a major role in determining the prosperscore

Prosperscore, loan status and employment status¶

The figure bellow confirms that, all employees with good loan status have higher prosper score and those with bad loan status have a lower prosper score.

Hence strong determinants of the prosperscore.

Conclusion¶

The following variables are strong determinants of the Prosperscore:

Loan status and Employment status: Full time workers with short term loans have a higher prosperscore

Loan categories and Loan Term: Household Expenses, Mdedical/Dental, Auto, Home improvement, large purchase with short term loans have a higher score

Incomegroup: High income earners have a higher Prosperscore

Loan status and Employment Status: Employees with good loan status have higher prosper score

Incomeverifiable: Employees with verifiable sources of income have a higher prosperscore
